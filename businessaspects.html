  • What is our Value Proposition?
    • Our state of the art technology accurately models various terrains using remote sensing, a method of collected data about a surface by scanning it using satellite.
    • We understand that rovers and other machinery can be incredibly complex, so our customer service team is always ready to help. Unlike bigger manufacturers, our smaller business can cater your experience to your concerns.

  • How will they distribute their product or service to customers?
