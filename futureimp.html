Future Implementations





















Our sensors will allow for rovers to examine the conditions of Mars such as

  • climate
  • geology
  • atmospheric pressure
  • planetary structure

    • After making our invention more safe and sustainable, we can implement our knowledge to help colonize Mars by sending space shuttles and conducting human exploration. Overtime, this will allow us to acheive a vast understanding of Mars and may ultimately lead to colonization and/or industrialization.
